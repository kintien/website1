About Santa Clara Fitness

Athletic programs starting at the High School level include a Strength and Conditioning component. We believe that youth athletics can and should develop these aspects of training. AOP has developed the standard for youth development for Physical Literacy, Mental Performance and Health/Nutrition.

Mental and Physical Health

Mastering the fundamental movements allow athletes to read their environment and make appropriate decisions. This will not only build confident motor control, but essentially prevent athletes from injury. We will also provide useful information for optimal athletic performance, including meal plans, nutritional education, and recipes.

Our Goal

About us

The Art of Play challenges and motivates student-athletes to push for new heights. We teach our students the proper technique for fundamental movement principles. Our Play-based FUNdamentals training is a combination of physical agility, mental conditioning, and just flat out having fun (Think Einstein in a jersey). With our enthusiastic coaching, and our positive training environment, every athlete will receive the level of attention required to improve his/her skill. We have experienced coaches in every major sport and we focus on complete athlete development. AOP is designed to help elevate your game for the next level. We are dedicated in providing to our athletes a fun and memorable training experience.
